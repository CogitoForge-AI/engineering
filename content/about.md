Cogito Engineering is the publishing space for engineers across Cogito to share
what we are learning while building products, AI systems, and internal
platforms.

This site exists to make engineering work more legible. We want to document not
just outcomes, but the decisions, tradeoffs, and constraints behind them.

We publish writing about:

- product engineering and architecture
- reliability, observability, and operations
- AI systems and evaluation
- tooling, developer experience, and internal platforms
- team practices that help us move with care and speed

The broader Cogito open-source presence lives under the `cogitoForge-AI`
GitHub organization, and this publication complements that work with context:
why we made certain choices, what surprised us, and what others can reuse.

The goal is simple: turn day-to-day engineering work into clear, reusable
knowledge for teammates, future collaborators, and the engineering community.
